Tele-Psychiatry

At RHC, Tele-Psychiatry prioritizes compassionate care alongside medical expertise, recognizing the impact of psychiatric conditions on patients and families.

  • Timely Access to Care: Swift access to psychiatric expertise reduces wait times, enabling early intervention for improved outcomes.
  • Remote Assessments and Diagnoses: Psychiatrists conduct remote assessments, facilitating prompt treatment planning, monitoring patient progress, and providing ongoing support through telepsychiatry platforms.

At the heart of RHC's Tele-Psychiatry service is a commitment to holistic care and patient well-being, bridging distances to deliver compassionate and comprehensive mental health support.

  • Compassionate Support: Tele-Psychiatry sessions offer a supportive environment for individuals to address concerns and receive care from experienced psychiatrists.
  • Empowerment Through Education: The program includes psychoeducation, empowering individuals to understand their conditions and participate in treatment decisions.
