Our Mission

At the core of our mission lies an unwavering commitment to ensuring equitable access to critical care services for hospitals across the nation, irrespective of their geographical location. Recognizing the inherent disparities that exist in healthcare access, particularly in underserved and remote areas, we are dedicated to bridging these gaps and ensuring that every community has access to life-saving medical resources.


Through strategic partnerships, innovative technology, and a steadfast dedication to excellence, we aim to establish a comprehensive network of critical care support that extends to even the most remote corners of our country

  • Equitable critical care access nationwide.
  • Leveraging telemedicine and mobile units
  • Collaborative partnerships with local providers
